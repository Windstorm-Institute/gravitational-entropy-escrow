## Cover note

This paper takes a self-consciously narrow approach: it is interpretive rather than derivational. The mathematical machinery underlying the reframing is due to Bekenstein, Hawking, Unruh, Jacobson, Padmanabhan, Verlinde, and van Putten; the contribution offered here is the physical picture that connects their results — that gravitational binding energy literally is escrowed entropy, exchanged at the local Unruh temperature against the cosmological horizon as thermodynamic floor.

The paper is explicit about what it does and does not deliver:

- It **recovers** Newton's law, Bekenstein–Hawking entropy, the equivalence principle, and the deep-MOND Tully–Fisher relation as facets of the same picture.
- It **does not derive** new field equations from first principles. The relativistic sketch (§7) identifies the Bianchi-respecting structural form `G_μν + Λ_eff(τ) g_μν = (8πG/c⁴) T_μν^(total)` and explicitly rejects the naive multiplicative form `G_μν + Λ g_μν = (8πG/c⁴) f(τ) T_μν` on Bianchi-and-equivalence grounds, but `Λ_eff(τ)` is not derived.
- It **engages quantitatively** with the empirical literature: a five-case extension of Milgrom (2017)'s test on Genzel et al. (2017) high-z galaxies independently disfavors `H(z)`-tracking of `a_0` (residual ≈ 0.11 dex) alongside the `(1+z)^(3/2)` exclusion (residual ≈ 0.18 dex), and a SPARC reanalysis (§8.4) gives a global deep-MOND `a_0 ≈ 1.24 × 10⁻¹⁰ m/s²`.
- It **acknowledges** that the bare Λ-form prediction `c√(Λ/3)/(2π) ≈ 9.0 × 10⁻¹¹ m/s²` lies ~25% below the SPARC value; the residual is treated as an order-unity coefficient `α ≈ 1.39` awaiting derivation, not absorbed by parameter-fitting.
- It **flags honestly** the radial cluster-core mass-discrepancy pattern as a real difficulty the framework cannot dissolve in its present form (§8.2).

We submit this work to CQG as appropriate for the journal's foundations-of-gravity readership. The posture is conservative: limitations are enumerated, scope is bounded, and adversarial review of the central interpretive claim is welcomed.
